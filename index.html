Our Research Focus

Fault-Tolerant Quantum Computing

Quantum bits (qubits) are notoriously fragile and susceptible to environmental noise, leading to errors in computation. Our primary research focuses on designing and implementing quantum error correction codes and fault-tolerant architectures.

We investigate novel qubit modalities, develop sophisticated control techniques, and create theoretical frameworks to protect quantum information and enable reliable, large-scale quantum computation. This is crucial for realizing the true potential of quantum algorithms in fields like drug discovery, materials science, and complex system simulation.
