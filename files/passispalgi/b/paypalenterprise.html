Certification Process for Payment UK Enterprises

英国支付企业认证流程

The certification process is divided into multiple detailed steps. The table below explains each step along with the operation and any payment details involved.

认证流程分为多个详细步骤。下表解释了每个步骤以及相关的操作和任何支付详情。

Process Steps 流程步骤
Step 步骤 Description 描述 Payment/Notes 付款/备注
Step 1 步骤 1 Input information according to the certification process. Use the business address as the home address. 根据认证流程输入信息。使用企业地址作为家庭地址。 None 无
Step 2 步骤 2 An error occurs during the application process due to a mistake in the input. 由于输入错误，申请过程中出现错误。 None 无
Step 3 步骤 3 Contact customer service to report and resolve the error. 联系客户服务以报告并解决错误。 None 无
Step 4 步骤 4 Receive a link from customer service to restart the authentication process. 从客户服务收到重新启动认证过程的链接。 None 无
Step 5 步骤 5 Re-authenticate through the provided link. 通过提供的链接重新认证。 None 无
Step 6 步骤 6 During re-authentication, bind your credit card from Construction Bank. 在重新认证过程中，绑定您的建设银行信用卡。 None 无
Step 7 步骤 7 Due to unfamiliarity with the operation, an extra fee is incurred, and binding has to be performed twice. 由于不熟悉操作流程，产生额外费用，需要进行两次绑定。 Two fees are charged for the credit card binding process. 信用卡绑定过程中收取两次费用。
Step 8 步骤 8 The total binding process costs $2, which is equivalent to 14 RMB. 绑定过程总共花费2美元，相当于14人民币。 $2 / 14 RMB 2美元 / 14人民币
Step 9 步骤 9 Pay the company's registration fee and the enterprise certification fee. 支付公司注册费和企业认证费。 Additional fees apply 需要额外费用
Step 10 步骤 10 The final cost consists of the 14 RMB for card binding plus the registration and certification fees. 最终费用包括卡绑定的14人民币加上注册费和认证费。 Total cost = 14 RMB + Registration Fee + Certification Fee 总费用 = 14人民币 + 注册费 + 认证费
